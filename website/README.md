Search for `[Your Address]` in:
- `privacy.html`
- `terms.html`

### Update Jurisdiction

In `terms.html`, search for `[Your Jurisdiction]` and replace with your actual jurisdiction (e.g., "the State of California" or "England and Wales").

### Modify App Store Links

When your app is live, replace `#` in the App Store badge links with your actual App Store URL:
```html
<a href="https://apps.apple.com/app/idYOUR_APP_ID">
```
