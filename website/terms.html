5. The Credit System

How Credits Work

Upon subscribing, you receive 7 credits at the start of each week. The credit system operates as follows:

  • Keep a Credit: Stay within your daily app usage limits
  • Lose a Credit: Exceed your daily limit for any monitored app
  • Weekly Reset: At week's end, you must return to 7 credits to continue
  • Credit Cost: Each lost credit costs $0.99

Credit Charges

  • You will be charged for lost credits at the end of each 7-day cycle
  • Charges are processed through Apple's App Store
  • You authorize us to charge your payment method for lost credits
  • Credits are consumable in-app purchases and cannot be refunded

Credit Purchases

You may purchase additional credits at any time through our in-app store:

  • 1 Credit: $0.99
  • 4 Credits: $3.99
  • 7 Credits: $6.99
