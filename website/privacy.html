Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 24, 2025

At SE7EN, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Screen Time Data

SE7EN uses Apple's Screen Time API to monitor your app usage. This data includes:

  • Apps you choose to monitor
  • Daily usage time for selected apps
  • Time limits you set for each app
  • Whether you stayed within your daily limits

Important: All screen time data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or have access to your screen time information on our servers.

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for account recovery and notifications)
  • Device identifier (for subscription management)
  • App preferences and settings

Payment Information

All payment transactions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store or have access to your payment card details. We only receive confirmation of:

  • Subscription status (active/inactive)
  • Purchase history (credit top-ups)
  • Transaction dates and amounts

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data to improve our app:

  • App crashes and performance metrics
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Device type and iOS version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Calculate your daily credit status based on your app usage goals
  • Manage Your Subscription: Process payments and maintain your credit balance
  • Send Notifications: Alert you when approaching usage limits or when credits are lost
  • Improve Our Service: Analyze app performance and user experience
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your screen time data and app usage information are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure storage APIs. This data is protected by your device's encryption and security features.

Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your goals and preferences may be stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted and only accessible to you across your devices.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to user data

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Analytics (anonymous usage data only)
  • Customer support
  • Email notifications

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as:

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Government investigations
  • Protection of our legal rights

Business Transfers

If SE7EN is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Access and Export

You can request a copy of your personal data stored on our servers at any time by contacting support@se7enapp.com.

Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data through the app's Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Opt-Out of Communications

You can disable push notifications in your device settings or within the app's notification preferences.

Do Not Track

We respect "Do Not Track" signals and do not track users across third-party websites.

6. Children's Privacy

SE7EN is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Apple Screen Time API

SE7EN uses Apple's Screen Time API, which is governed by Apple's privacy policies. Screen time data accessed through this API remains on your device and is subject to Apple's security and privacy standards.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. After account deletion:

  • Screen time data: Deleted immediately (stored locally only)
  • Account information: Retained for 30 days for recovery purposes, then permanently deleted
  • Transaction records: Retained for 7 years for legal and accounting purposes

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending you an in-app notification

Your continued use of SE7EN after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

Note: SE7EN does not sell personal information.

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent
