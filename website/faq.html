Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SE7EN

General Questions

What is SE7EN? ▼
SE7EN is a credit-based accountability app that helps you reduce screen time addiction. You start each week with 7 credits. Stay within your daily app usage limits to keep your credits. Exceed your limits and lose credits. At the end of each week, you pay $0.99 for each credit lost. This financial accountability helps you stay disciplined with your screen time goals.
How does the credit system work? ▼
You receive 7 credits at the start of each week when you subscribe. Each day, you set time limits for specific apps (like Instagram, TikTok, etc.). If you stay within your limits for the day, you keep your credit. If you exceed the limit, you lose one credit for that day. At the end of the week, you're charged $0.99 for each lost credit, and your credits reset to 7 for the next week.
Why does SE7EN cost money? ▼
SE7EN uses financial accountability as a psychological tool. Research shows that loss aversion (the fear of losing money) is a powerful motivator for behavior change. When there's a real cost to exceeding your limits, you're much more likely to stick to your goals. The base $7/week subscription grants you 7 credits, and you only pay extra ($0.99 per credit) if you break your commitments.
Is SE7EN available for Android? ▼
Currently, SE7EN is only available for iOS (iPhone and iPad). We're focusing on delivering the best possible experience on Apple devices first. Android support may come in the future based on demand.

Pricing & Billing

How much does SE7EN cost? ▼
SE7EN has a base subscription of $7.00 per week, which includes access to all features and 7 credits. Additionally, you'll be charged $0.99 for each credit you lose during the week. For example, if you lose 2 credits in a week, your total charge would be $7.00 (subscription) + $1.98 (2 lost credits) = $8.98 for that week.
Can I buy extra credits? ▼
Yes! You can purchase credit top-ups at any time through the in-app store:
  • 1 Credit: $0.99
  • 4 Credits: $3.99 (save $0.04)
  • 7 Credits: $6.99 (best value, save $0.94)
These purchases can help you maintain your credit balance if you've had a difficult week.
When will I be charged? ▼
Your subscription renews weekly on the same day you first subscribed. Charges for lost credits are processed at the end of each 7-day cycle. For example, if you subscribed on Monday, you'll be charged every Monday for the base subscription plus any credits lost during the previous week.
Can I get a refund? ▼
All charges are final and non-refundable. This is part of the accountability system - the commitment you make is binding. However, we may provide refunds at our sole discretion in cases of technical errors, unauthorized purchases (reported within 48 hours), or extended service outages. Contact support@se7enapp.com for exceptional circumstances.
How do I cancel my subscription? ▼
You can cancel anytime through your iPhone's Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > SE7EN > Cancel Subscription. Your cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. You'll still be charged for any credits lost before cancellation. No refunds are provided for the current period.

Features & Usage

Which apps can I monitor? ▼
You can monitor any app installed on your iPhone using Apple's Screen Time API. Popular choices include social media apps (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook), entertainment apps (YouTube, Netflix), games, and more. You choose which apps to monitor and set individual time limits for each.
How accurate is the tracking? ▼
SE7EN uses Apple's official Screen Time API, which is the same technology that powers iOS's built-in Screen Time feature. It's highly accurate and tracks actual app usage time. The tracking runs in the background and doesn't require any manual input from you.
Can I change my daily limits? ▼
Yes! You can adjust your daily limits at any time in the Goals section. However, changes only apply to future days - you can't retroactively change a limit to avoid losing a credit for the current day. This maintains the integrity of the accountability system.
Will SE7EN block apps? ▼
No, SE7EN does not block or restrict access to your apps. It's an accountability tool, not a blocking tool. You'll receive notifications when you're approaching your limits and when you exceed them, but you maintain full control. The accountability comes from the financial consequence of losing credits, not from forced restrictions.
Do I get notifications? ▼
Yes! SE7EN sends helpful notifications to keep you on track:
  • Warning at 75% of your daily limit
  • Alert when you reach your limit
  • Notification when you lose a credit
  • Daily summary of your progress
  • Weekly credit balance updates
You can customize notification preferences in Settings.

Privacy & Security

Is my screen time data private? ▼
Absolutely. Your screen time data is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure APIs. We do not transmit, store, or have access to your app usage information on our servers. Only you can see which apps you use and for how long.
What data do you collect? ▼
We only collect the minimum data necessary to operate the service:
  • Email address (for account management)
  • Subscription status (managed through Apple)
  • Credit balance and transaction history
  • Anonymous usage analytics (app crashes, feature usage)
We never see your actual screen time data or which specific apps you use. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I delete my data? ▼
Yes. You can delete your account and all associated data anytime through Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account, credit history, and preferences. Screen time data is already stored only on your device, so deleting the app removes that as well. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Troubleshooting

The app isn't tracking my usage. What should I do? ▼
If tracking isn't working, check these settings:
  1. Open iPhone Settings > Screen Time
  2. Ensure Screen Time is enabled
  3. Open Settings > SE7EN
  4. Ensure "Screen Time" permission is granted
  5. Restart the SE7EN app
If problems persist, contact support@se7enapp.com with your device model and iOS version.
I was charged incorrectly. How do I dispute it? ▼
If you believe you were charged incorrectly:
  1. Check your credit history in the app (Credits tab)
  2. Verify the charges match your lost credits
  3. If there's an error, email support@se7enapp.com within 48 hours
  4. Include your transaction ID and explanation
We'll review and respond within 5 business days. For App Store billing issues, you may also contact Apple Support directly.
The app keeps crashing. Help! ▼
Try these steps:
  1. Force quit the app (swipe up from app switcher)
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Check for SE7EN updates in the App Store
  4. Ensure you're running iOS 16.0 or later
  5. Reinstall the app (your data is saved to your account)
If crashes continue, please report them to support@se7enapp.com with details about when they occur.
